FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate compound interest?+
Use the formula A = P(1 + r/n)^(nt), where P is principal, r is annual rate, n is compounds per year, and t is years. Our compound interest calculator does this instantly and shows a year-by-year breakdown.
How is BMI calculated?+
BMI = weight(kg) ÷ height(m)². For example, a person weighing 70kg at 1.75m has a BMI of 70 ÷ (1.75 × 1.75) = 22.9, which is in the healthy range of 18.5–24.9.
How do I calculate a monthly loan payment?+
Monthly payment = P × [r(1+r)^n] / [(1+r)^n – 1], where P is the loan principal, r is the monthly interest rate (annual rate ÷ 12), and n is the total number of payments. Use our loan calculator to get the result instantly.
